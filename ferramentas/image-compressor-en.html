Why compress images?

Large images are the main cause of website slowness and take up unnecessary space in emails and storage. Intelligent compression removes redundant data from the file without drastically affecting the visual quality that the human eye can perceive.

Total Privacy

Unlike other sites, we do not send your photos to any server. All compression happens instantly inside your browser (phone or computer). Your photos never leave your device.

Supported Formats

  • JPG/JPEG: The best format for photos with many colors. Allows high compression.
  • PNG: Ideal for graphics, logos, and images with transparency.
  • WEBP: The modern web format offering superior quality at smaller sizes.
