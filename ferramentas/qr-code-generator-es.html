What is a QR Code?

A QR Code (Quick Response Code) is a two-dimensional barcode that can be easily scanned using most camera-equipped mobile phones. Unlike traditional barcodes that only store numbers, QR Codes can store information such as website addresses (URLs), text, phone numbers, and even Wi-Fi access data.

Security and Privacy

Our tool runs 100% in your browser. This means the content you type to generate the code is never sent to our servers, ensuring total privacy of your data.

What is a QR Code for?

The versatility of QR Codes makes them useful in various everyday situations and business applications:

  • Digital Menus: Restaurants use them to avoid physical menus.
  • Easy Wi-Fi: Allows connection to the network without typing complex passwords.
  • Payments: Widely used in payment systems globally.
  • Marketing: Product packaging linking to videos or manuals.

Static vs. Dynamic QR Code

The code generated by this tool is of the Static type. This means the information (such as your website link) is "embedded" directly into the image pattern.

The main advantage is that it never expires and works forever, with no monthly fees. The disadvantage is that if you need to change the link, you will have to generate and print a new code.
