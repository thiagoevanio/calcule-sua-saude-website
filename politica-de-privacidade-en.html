Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 24, 2024

Calcule Sua Saúde values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy transparently explains how we collect, use, share, and protect the information of users who access our website.

Our Commitment

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. All collected information, such as calculator results or quiz answers, is used exclusively to improve your experience on the site or processed anonymously for statistical purposes.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Voluntarily Provided Data: Information you enter in our contact forms, newsletter, calculators (such as weight, height, age), or quizzes.
  • Navigation Data: Information collected automatically, such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent, and device used.

2. Use of Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and personalize calculator and quiz results.
  • To improve the functionality and content of the website.
  • To send newsletters and updates (only if you have opted in to receive them).
  • To analyze traffic trends for technical optimization.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to improve your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device.

Types of Cookies we use:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the basic functioning of the website (e.g., light/dark theme preferences).
  • Analytics Cookies: Allow us to understand how visitors interact with the site (e.g., Google Analytics).

You can configure your browser to refuse cookies, but this may limit some website functionalities.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with companies, organizations, or individuals outside of Calcule Sua Saúde, except in the following cases:

  • With your consent: We will share personal information only when we have your explicit authorization.
  • For legal reasons: If we are required by law, regulation, or legal process.

5. Data Security

We adopt appropriate technical and administrative security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights (LGPD)

In accordance with the General Data Protection Law (LGPD - Brazil), you have the right to:

  • Confirm the existence of data processing.
  • Access the data we hold about you.
  • Correct incomplete, inaccurate, or outdated data.
  • Request anonymization, blocking, or deletion of unnecessary data.
  • Revoke your consent at any time.

7. Changes to this Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy periodically. We recommend that you review this page regularly to be aware of any changes. The date of the last update will always be visible at the top of this page.

8. Contact the Data Protection Officer (DPO)

To exercise your rights or if you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact our Data Protection Officer:

Email: privacidade@calculesuasaude.com.br
Address: São Paulo, SP - Brazil
