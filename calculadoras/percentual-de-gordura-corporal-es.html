Why measure Porcentaje de Grasa Corporal?

Weight on the scale doesn't tell the whole story. Two people with the same weight and height can look and feel completely different depending on their body composition (muscle to fat ratio).

About the U.S. Navy Method

This method estimates body density using equations based on circumferences. While not as precise as a DEXA scan or hydrostatic weighing, it is considered the most reliable home method, with an acceptable margin of error of 3-4% when measurements are taken correctly.
